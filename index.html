Ping-Pong

A Ping-Pong Unlike Any Other Ping-Pong You've Played

The Game:

  1. Input a number
  2. We'll count up to that number BUT...
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced by 'ping'
  4. Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced by 'pong'
  5. And numbers divisible by 15 will be replaced by 'ping-pong'
